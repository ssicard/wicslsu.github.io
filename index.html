About

48 hours

The Women in Computer Science Game Jam is an event that takes place from Friday, November 6th at 5PM to Sunday, November 8th at 6PM. You have 48 hours to create a video game, and like most game jams, this means staying overnight (or as late as you can!) to finish your game. Game jams are intense, exciting, and a ton of fun!

create video games

You can create any kind of game you want, and we're going to announce a theme the day of the event to provide inspiration. WICS wants to help you earn game development, team dynamic, and project experience from this event. You do not need game development experience, but experience in coding, art, or music helps! We recommend you use GameMaker if you don't already have experience in any relevant skills. There will also be tutorials on fast game development provided the day of the event.

who can participate

This event is hosted by Women in Computer Science at LSU, but students of any gender can participate. You can come with or without a team- we'll match you up with people if you don't have one already! We also need volunteers to help out the day of the event. Register below if you're interested in participating or volunteering!

Women in Computer Science at LSU

Women in Computer Science at LSU was founded by 7 LSU computer science students during the 2015 Spring semester. Our mission is to provide a community, both academically, professionally, and socially for women studying computer science at LSU. We hold regular tutoring sessions, professional workshops, socials, and other events, all of which are open to LSU students. We're always looking for new members and sponsors; if you're interested, contact wicslsu@gmail.com.
