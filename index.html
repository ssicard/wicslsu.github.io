About

Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes

This past summer, I worked for EA Mobile on a new collectible character game called Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes. I wrote a tool for Unity3D to help QA and designers test the battle systems.

Wanted

During the 2015 Global Game Jam, a team of 8 people, including myself, created an interactive narrative called Wanted. As the only team member with game development experience, I did my best to help the team learn and create something they enjoyed creating.

Narrative in Animation Project (WIP)

In my ART 4020: Narrative in Animation class, my team of 4 people is creating an interactive 3D short about birds in love. I'm teaching myself Maya and technical art and blogging about it along the way.
